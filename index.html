  2. Train

  3. Training of Machine Learning & Computer Vision Models is a task that has gradually become within the means of any individual today thanks to the many tools available that significantly makes it easy. In this time, any aspiring ML enginner can build models easily even without hardware resources that can handle heavy processing. Aside considering the hardware side of it, actually building models can't be said to be difficult, it's more like learning sets of tools and libraries that you need at different times to plug in.
